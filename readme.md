97% of cold emails go straight to the trash because they look like they were sent to 500 other people.

To fix this, I built an AI agent that researches first, and writes second.

Day 4 of 100: ColdOutreach AI 🎯

Give it a prospect name + your offer. In 60 seconds, it gives you:

🔍 Deep prospect research (pain points, triggers)
📬 5 subject lines (predicting 40%+ open rates)
✉️ 3 highly personalized email variants
📅 A 3-touch follow-up sequence
🛡️ Objection handling prep for their top 4 pushbacks

No templates. Every line references something specific about that prospect.

Built with Node.js & Claude API.
