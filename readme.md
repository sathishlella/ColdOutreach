# ColdOutreach AI

> **Research Prospects → Write Hyper-Personalized Cold Emails**

A premium AI-powered cold outreach sequence generator that transforms prospect research into multi-variant personalized email campaigns. Built for founders, sales teams, and growth-focused professionals who want to land meetings with the right people.

## 🎯 What It Does

ColdOutreach AI takes your prospect details and value proposition, then generates:

✓ **5 High-Converting Subject Lines** — A/B tested variations with open rate predictions  
✓ **3 Email Variants** — Different hooks (storytelling, direct, question-based) for optimal engagement  
✓ **Personalization Hooks** — Custom intel integration + company-specific signals  
✓ **Follow-up Sequence** — Day 3 bump, Day 7 value-add, Day 14 breakup with scripts  
✓ **Signal Detection** — Identifies enterprise budgets, hiring signals, pain points automatically  

All generated copy is ready to paste and send—no editing required.

## 📖 How to Use

### 1. Enter Prospect Details
Fill in the left panel:
- **Name, Title, Company** — Who you're reaching out to
- **Industry & Size** — Context for positioning
- **LinkedIn URL** (optional) — For future integrations
- **Pain Points / Intel** — Your research advantage

### 2. Define Your Offer
- **What You're Selling** — Your product/service
- **Value Proposition** — Key benefits (copy-pasted into emails)
- **Email Tone** — Your outreach personality
- **CTA Style** — How you want to end the email

### 3. Generate
Click **Generate Outreach Sequence** and watch the AI:
1. Research the prospect
2. Analyze company signals
3. Build personalization hooks
4. Write email variants
5. Score & optimize
6. Plan follow-up sequence

Entire salvo ready in <3 seconds.

### 4. Copy & Deploy
- Click any subject line to copy
- Click any email variant to copy
- Share your follow-up dates with your CRM
- Send with confidence

## 💡 Pro Tips

**Research First** — The better your intel, the better your emails:
- Check recent tweets/LinkedIn activity
- Note hiring announcements
- Spot pain signals (complaints, outages)
- Find mutual connections

**Test Variants** — Send different opens to similar prospects:
- Email A to relationship-focused buyers
- Email B to time-constrained executives
- Email C to consultative decision-makers

**Personalize Further** — The generated copy is a foundation:
- Add specific accomplishments you've noticed
- Reference their recent hiring/fundraising
- Mention mutual connections
- Update follow-up dates per your calendar
