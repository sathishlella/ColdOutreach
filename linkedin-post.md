**Day 4 of 100: ColdOutreach AI** 🎯

Here's what it does:

You give it a prospect name + your offer.

In 60 seconds, it gives you back:

🔍 Deep prospect research — pain points, buying signals, personalization hooks
📬 5 subject lines — each with open rate prediction (we're hitting 40%+)
✉️ 3 email variants:
  → Variant A: Storytelling approach
  → Variant B: Punchy & direct (under 80 words)
  → Variant C: Question hook
📅 3-touch follow-up sequence — bump, value-add, and breakup email
🛡️ Objection handling — the 4 things they'll push back on + your response

Every single line references something specific about THAT prospect.

No templates. No "I hope this email finds you well."

The secret? The agent researches first, writes second.

Just like the best SDRs do — except it takes 60 seconds instead of 45 minutes.
