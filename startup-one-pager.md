## The Problem

Sales reps spend 45 minutes researching each prospect and still write generic emails. The average cold email reply rate is 1-3%. SDRs send 50-100 emails/day, but 97% go straight to trash because they feel like spam. The gap isn't effort — it's personalization at scale. Nobody can deeply research AND write personalized emails AND maintain a follow-up cadence for 100 prospects simultaneously.

**$1.7 trillion** is spent annually on B2B sales — and the first touchpoint (cold email) fails 97% of the time.

---

## The Solution: ColdOutreach AI

An AI agent that takes a prospect name + your offer and autonomously generates a complete personalized outreach sequence in under 60 seconds.

**Input:** Prospect details (name, title, company) + your product/offer

**Output:**
- **Prospect Research** — pain points, buying signals, personalization hooks, likely objections
- **5 A/B Subject Lines** — each with open rate prediction and strategy
- **3 Email Variants** — Storytelling, Direct, and Question-Hook approaches
- **3-Touch Follow-Up Sequence** — Day 3 bump, Day 7 value-add, Day 14 breakup
- **Objection Handling Guide** — top 4 objections with word-for-word responses

Every email references specific details about the prospect. No templates. No filler.

## Go-To-Market Strategy

### Phase 1 — Viral PLG (Months 1–3)
- Free tier with "Powered by ColdOutreach AI" in email signature
- LinkedIn content series (Day 4 of 100!)
- "Cold Email Roast" feature — paste your current cold email, we rewrite it (viral loop)
- Product Hunt launch targeting sales category

### Phase 2 — Integrations (Months 4–9)
- Chrome extension: visit LinkedIn profile → one click → full sequence generated
- Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive integrations
- Zapier/Make: trigger sequence when new lead added to CRM
- Partner with Lemlist, Instantly, Outreach for sequence import

### Phase 3 — Intelligence Layer (Months 10–18)
- "What's working" dashboard — real-time analytics on open/reply rates by approach
- Auto-optimize: AI learns which variant style works best per industry/role
- Team playbooks: SDR managers create and enforce winning templates
- Seed raise: $2M at $18M valuation
